History of the Keep Calm Poster

The Keep Calm poster was born in Britian in 1939 as a morale booster during WWII

It was thought the posters were destroyed until they were rediscovered almost 60 years later. They live on today as a Meme.

Activity X

Made by Mozilla X and remixed for Clubs by jgmac1106 Mozilla.

Learners will create a "Keep Calm" poster to celebrate back to school. They will remix HTML to include a message about how to succeed in school. Learners can also remix the CSS to change the look of the poster.
